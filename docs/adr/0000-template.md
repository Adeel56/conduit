## Context

What is the situation? What forces are at play (technical, product, time, cost)?
What problem are we actually solving? Keep it factual.

## Decision

What did we decide to do? State it in one or two clear sentences.

## Options considered

1. **<Option A>** — pros / cons.
2. **<Option B>** — pros / cons.
3. **<Option chosen>** — pros / cons. *(mark which one)*

Listing what we **rejected** and **why** is the most valuable part of an ADR.

## Consequences

- **Positive:** what gets better.
- **Negative / trade-offs:** what we accept as the cost.
- **Follow-ups:** anything this forces us to do later.

## Reversal

If this decision turns out wrong, how do we back out? Is it cheap or expensive to reverse?
A decision that is expensive to reverse deserves more scrutiny up front.
